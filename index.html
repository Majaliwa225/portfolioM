H2 ICE Integrated System

A next-gen hydrogen internal combustion engine (H2-ICE) system engineered to generate clean, off-grid electricity. This project integrates Waste Heat Recovery Systems (WHRS) and an Exhaust Heat Recovery Unit to boost thermal efficiency and reduce fuel losses. Designed as a scalable micro power plant for rural or decentralized applications, the system captures exhaust heat to produce additional power or thermal energy—maximizing sustainability, minimizing waste.

Key Features:

1. Hydrogen-fueled ICE core for clean combustion and near-zero carbon emissions

2. Integrated WHRS for recovering and reusing engine heat losses

3. Exhaust Heat Recovery Unit for powering auxiliary systems or preheating processes

Automated Irrigation System

A precision irrigation solution designed to optimize water usage using real-time soil moisture and environmental sensors. The system automates watering schedules based on live data, reducing water waste and improving crop health. Powered by microcontrollers (Arduino/PIC/etc.), the setup includes sensors, relays, a water pump, and a control logic system—engineered to serve both smallholder and commercial farms.

Key Features:

1. Soil moisture sensing

2. Automated pump activation & valve control

3. Low-power design for solar integration

4. Customizable thresholds and irrigation logic

5. Scalable for gardens, greenhouses, and open fields

DUSAC-Organization

DUSAC is a Pan-African industrial initiative dedicated to uniting essential systems that fuel economic resilience and technological independence across the continent. Its core mission is to establish and run integrated industries in key sectors—Food & Beverages, Medical & Health, Electronics Assembly, and Education & Scientific Labs—while generating and managing its own sustainable power. Milk-LA VIDA

Milk La-Vida is a modern milk distribution brand dedicated to delivering fresh, hygienic, and accessible dairy products using an automated, tech-powered model. Built on quality, convenience, and community health, the system integrates milk pasteurization, cold storage, and ATM-style vending machines to ensure customers get clean milk 24/7 — without middlemen or spoilage risks. Operating on a decentralized model, Milk La-Vida empowers local dairy farmers by purchasing directly from them, processing the milk at hygienic micro-units, and vending it in urban and peri-urban areas through solar-powered milk ATMs. Customers pay per liter using mobile money, making it both cashless and accessible.

Uniqueness:

1. Direct Farm-to-Cup Delivery

2. Cold Chain Guarantee

3. ATM Vending + Mobile Payment

4. Low Waste, High Efficiency

5. Community-Focused

The Future of H2-ICE: What's Coming Next?

As we move deeper into 2025, I’m energized by the rapid evolution of clean energy systems. Hydrogen Internal Combustion Engines (H₂-ICE) are reshaping how we approach sustainable power — offering a practical, low-carbon alternative to traditional fossil-fueled engines. With integrated waste heat recovery and compatibility with decentralized energy systems, H₂-ICE brings a powerful balance between familiar engineering and futuristic fuel. The mission now is to scale this tech in a way that aligns with industrial needs — keeping efficiency, reliability, and accessibility at the core.
